Troubleshooting

App not loading? Try the following steps:

  • Ensure your device is connected to the internet.
  • Restart the app.
  • Check if updates are available on the App Store.

Experiencing crashes? Please uninstall and reinstall the app. If the issue persists, contact us for support.
